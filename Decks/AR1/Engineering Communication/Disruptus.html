Activity

How to Play

I'll show you an image of an object, or two objects, and you'll be asked to do one of the following:

  1. 1
    Use it: imagine a new way this object could be used.
  2. 2
    Improve it: modify the object to make it more effective, efficient, or fun to use for its current purpose.
  3. 3
    Transform it: change the object in some way to solve a completely different problem.
  4. 4
    Combine it: merge two unrelated objects or ideas into a single new invention.
Sketch your innovation as clearly as you can. Give it a name, label its parts, and describe what it does and why it's useful.
